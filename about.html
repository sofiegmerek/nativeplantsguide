About This Site!


Hi my name is Sofie, I am a computer science student at UCSD and I created this website.
I've always had an interest in plants and this year I helped plan the redo of my house's front and back yard.
We transformed it from a lawn to a native and drought tolerant garden and got a $3000 rebate from the county.
I hope to help others learn about native plants and maybe transform their lawns as well with things I wish I knew.
